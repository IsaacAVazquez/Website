# Hero Section Component

## Purpose
Large, attention-grabbing section typically used at the top of pages to introduce content and drive action.

---

## Basic Hero Template

```markdown
# [Primary Headline]
## [Secondary Headline or Tagline]

[Brief value proposition or description (1-2 sentences)]

[Call to Action Button] • [Secondary Action]
```

---

## Example: Homepage Hero

```markdown
# Isaac Vazquez

## MBA Candidate • Product & Tech • Builder • Political Tech Background

I build products that bridge technology and human impact.

Currently pursuing my MBA at **UC Berkeley Haas**, where I'm deepening my product management expertise while exploring venture capital opportunities in civic tech and SaaS.
```

## Styling Guidelines

- **H1:** Display L (48px), Bold, Warm Gray 800
- **H2:** Heading 2 (32px), Semibold, Warm Gray 700
- **Description:** Body Large (18px), Regular, Warm Gray 600
- **CTA Buttons:** Primary style (Sunset Orange background)
- **Spacing:** Space 4XL (96px) section padding

---

## Best Practices

✅ **DO:**
- Keep headline under 60 characters
- Use action-oriented language
- Include clear call-to-action
- Add visual element (image, photo, illustration)
- Ensure mobile responsiveness

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use more than 2 CTAs
- Write overly long descriptions (>3 sentences)
- Skip the value proposition
- Use generic stock photos
- Forget alt text for images
