## Styling Guidelines

- **Background:** Warm Gray 800 or Warm Gray 900
- **Text Color:** Warm Gray 100 (light text on dark background)
- **Link Color:** Golden Yellow (#F7B32B) or Warm White
- **Link Hover:** Sunset Orange (#FF6B35)
- **Spacing:** Space 3XL (64px) padding top/bottom
- **Section Dividers:** Subtle line (1px, Warm Gray 700)

---

## Content Guidelines

### Essential Elements
- **Copyright:** Year and name
- **Contact:** Primary email or contact link
- **Navigation:** Key site sections
- **Social Links:** 3-5 primary social profiles

### Optional Elements
- Newsletter signup
- Current status/availability
- Site credits (built with, hosted on)
- Legal links (Privacy, Terms)
- Tagline or mission statement

## Accessibility Notes

- Ensure sufficient color contrast (WCAG AA minimum)
- Use semantic HTML (`<footer>` tag)
- Provide skip navigation for screen readers
- Make all links keyboard-accessible
- Use descriptive link text (not "click here")
