This release was part growth work and part product cleanup.

[/march-madness-2026](/march-madness-2026) was repackaged as a search-oriented editorial landing page with canonical metadata, article, FAQ, and sports structured data, a route-level Open Graph image, and query-param deep links for primary views and sub-tabs. The page also gained a stronger above-the-fold thesis, upset summaries, methodology cards, a share layer, and a companion writing piece.

At the site level, the global shell moved back to a simpler single-row navigation, closing CTAs were cleaned up, compact and full footer variants were added, and investments plus March Madness got full-width route handling so app-like surfaces were not trapped inside the default article wrapper.

The investments workspace got a UI pass at the same time, with a steadier page shell, cleaner tab hierarchy, better control-row spacing, and more coverage around layout width and mobile overflow.
