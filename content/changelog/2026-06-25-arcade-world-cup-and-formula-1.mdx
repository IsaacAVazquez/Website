This was the most intentionally off-brand release in a while:

- [Arcade](/arcade) launched with Reactor, a synthwave CRT reflex game built as a standalone style experiment inside the portfolio. It has its own font stack, visual system, and sitemap entry.
- World Cup coverage expanded with group-stage articles and matching sitemap updates, on top of the contender series and tournament dashboard that shipped earlier in June.
- [Formula 1 Pulse](/formula-1) got a racing-flavored visual identity pass so it feels less like a generic dashboard wearing F1 labels.
- The 404 page got a retro treatment, which is the right amount of personality for a page you only see when something went wrong.

The sitemap cleanup continued too, including removal of stale `/portfolio/pulse-dashboards` references.
