The writing archive got a practical new layer: build-breakdown pieces for 24 portfolio projects. These are not case studies trying to make everything sound bigger than it is. They explain what each tool does, why I built it, where the data comes from, and what tradeoffs shaped the implementation.

A few product surfaces moved at the same time:

- [Contact](/contact) got shorter and less repetitive, with the old "not a fit" section removed.
- [Formula 1 Pulse](/formula-1) got its first visual redesign pass, with a more dynamic dashboard treatment.
- Fantasy football snapshots now surface freshness more clearly and push with a sturdier workflow.
- SpaceX Mission Control started authenticating against Launch Library where possible and added a freshness gate so stale launch data is easier to spot.
- Header alignment got a small baseline fix so the brand wordmark and nav links sit together correctly.

This release made the site feel more explainable. More surfaces now have the "how it was built" layer next to the thing itself.
