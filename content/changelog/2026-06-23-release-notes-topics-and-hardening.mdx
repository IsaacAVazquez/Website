This was a site-operations release as much as a product release. [The changelog](/changelog) is now the canonical home for month-grouped release notes, and writing topic pages let the archive be browsed by recurring themes instead of only by date.

I improved findability across the homepage, header, live tools, and search experience, with search becoming easier to reach from the primary shell. Analytics and SEO picked up GA4 event tracking, a reference page for analytics events, and sitemap priority and changefreq metadata by content type.

The hardening work added a staged report-only CSP, stricter auth handling, safer JSON-LD output, and email rate limiting. I also kept large sports snapshots out of client bundles, trimmed image runtime, fixed mobile-menu focus order, repaired the compare-modal focus trap, and made filter state clearer to assistive tech.

The docs also got a maintenance pass, including the Spring 2026 roadmap, a shorter `CLAUDE.md`, and a prompting best-practices guide.
