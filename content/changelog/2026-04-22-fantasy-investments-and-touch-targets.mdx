The fantasy football tool surface got a broad rework as the board and draft-assistant ideas continued to settle.

Investments gained clearer data freshness messaging and a harder refresh pipeline, which matters because the page blends local portfolio state, live quotes, and curated historical snapshots. When those sources have different update cadences, the UI has to say that plainly.

The same pass fixed dark-mode elevation and touch-target compliance issues. That kind of polish is easy to dismiss until a small control becomes hard to hit on a phone or a raised surface breaks in dark mode.
