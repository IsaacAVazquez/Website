Two building blocks landed on the [investments surface](/investments):

- **`MetricTooltip`** — hover explanations for each metric so the numbers don't just sit there without context.
- **`ResearchSidebar`** — a side panel for digging into a symbol without leaving the portfolio view.

Layout and styling got a small pass at the same time, and the researched symbol now clears on navigation instead of sticking around when you move to another view.

The stock search dropdown also got a little more breathing room.
