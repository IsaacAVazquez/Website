Launched the [MBA internship notifications dashboard](/mba-internship-notifications) — a tool I built for my own recruiting cycle that was useful enough to share with the cohort.

Under the hood:

- Role aggregation with per-company filters.
- Email notifications via Resend.
- A lightweight admin layer for curating the source list.

The goal is narrow: reduce the number of tabs I have open during recruiting season. If it's useful to you, great.
