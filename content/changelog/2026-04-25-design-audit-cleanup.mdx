This was the cleanup pass that made the late-April redesign possible.

The audit backlog covered the shell, writing, dashboards, fantasy, fintech, and the MBA tracker. Fixes landed across empty states, polling announcements, tab accessibility, dropdown polish, URL state, backoff behavior, validation, pagination, and dashboard detail work.

The important part was not that every issue was dramatic. It was that the site had become broad enough that small inconsistencies were starting to compound, and the audit gave those issues a place to be closed instead of rediscovered.
