The writing archive got a stronger browsing layer with `WritingArchiveV3`. The goal was to make the archive feel useful once the article count got high enough that a simple reverse-chronological list was no longer doing enough work.

Shared error handling and logging were cleaned up at the same time. That kind of refactor does not change the look of a page, but it changes how quickly failures can be understood when a route or component breaks.
