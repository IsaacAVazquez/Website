This was a sweep through the places where fast shipping had left rough edges:

- Correctness fixes landed across the snapshot builders, including Formula 1, GitHub Trending, investments, SpaceX, La Liga, MLB, NBA, and NFL.
- Crash and state fixes landed across personal tools and dashboards, including earthquake, food map, museum log, polling, recipe finder, tech startup tracker, and the retirement planner.
- SEO and feed cleanup keeps title de-duplication from stripping the homepage title, keeps RSS output valid, and keeps search-route metadata aligned with the actual page behavior.
- The MBA email path got stricter handling around the digest API, plus a handful of stronger tests around recipes and wine-cellar parsing.

It is not the kind of release that changes the first impression of the site, but it changes how much I trust the site after I have been away from a page for a week.
