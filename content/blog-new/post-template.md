## Hook / Opening

Start with a compelling hook that:
- Identifies the problem or question you're addressing
- Explains why this matters to the reader
- Promises value or insight
- Sets the tone for the article

Use 2-4 paragraphs to draw the reader in. Be specific about what they'll learn or gain from reading this article.
