# Content Plan: AI for MBAs & Product Managers (3-Month, 24 Posts)

**Last updated:** 2026-04-07
**Cadence:** 2 posts/week — 1 flagship (2–3k words) + 1 lighter tactical (800–1200 words)
**Goal:** Build topical authority across 3 clusters; set up talks, courses, or paid products

---

## Cluster 1: "AI for MBAs & Product Managers" (Month 1, Weeks 1–4)

Positioning: MBA/PM who uses AI daily for strategy, research, and shipping products.

### Week 1
- **Flagship:** "How I Actually Use AI as an MBA and Aspiring PM (Day-to-Day Workflow)" — stack, readings, cases, recruiting, side projects; screenshots and time-savings examples
- **Tactical:** "The New Baseline: AI Skills Every PM Internship Will Expect in 2026" — how PM job postings now mention AI; skills by area (research, specs, analytics, experimentation)

### Week 2
- **Flagship:** "AI-Powered User Research: From 10 Interviews to Themes in One Evening" — full workflow: record → transcribe → synthesize → theme and prioritize; where AI helps vs where you still need judgment
- **Tactical:** "Designing Better PRDs with AI: Prompts, Structure, and Pitfalls" — tools like Productboard/Aha!/Craft.io; prompt patterns; examples of good vs bad AI-drafted sections

### Week 3
- **Flagship:** "Using AI for Quant & Data Cases: Building Models Faster Without Cheating" — using AI to generate model structures, check logic, pressure-test assumptions; what you never outsource
- **Tactical:** "From Inbox Chaos to Signal: AI for PM Email and Stakeholder Comms" — how PM-oriented tools help with user feedback, stakeholder updates, follow-ups

### Week 4
- **Flagship:** "Roadmapping with AI: Turning Raw Feedback into Quarterly Bets" — take real input (surveys, tickets, interviews); show how AI helps cluster themes, estimate impact, propose roadmap options
- **Tactical:** "Common Ways MBAs Misuse AI (and How to Stand Out Instead)" — critique shallow use; show thinking leverage vs shortcutting; examples from personal experience

## Cluster 2: "Agentic AI & Workflows for Builders and Small Teams" (Month 2, Weeks 5–8)

Positioning: PM/strategist who can design and critique agentic workflows.

### Week 5
- **Flagship:** "Agentic AI in Plain English: What It Is and Why PMs Should Care" — agents vs chatbots; planning, tools, memory, interfaces; simple examples
- **Tactical:** "Designing Your First Agentic Workflow: A Real Example from My Own Work" — personal use case; architecture, edge cases, failure modes

### Week 6
- **Flagship:** "AI Agents for Customer Support: What Actually Works vs Deckware" — real support use cases; KPIs (deflection, CSAT); where PMs should be skeptical
- **Tactical:** "Marketing & SEO on Autopilot? Testing Agentic Content Workflows" — agentic content pipeline (topic → outline → draft → optimize → publish); metrics and issues

### Week 7
- **Flagship:** "Multi-Agent Architectures for Business Workflows (Without the Hype)" — design patterns (planner, executor, critic, orchestrator); concrete business example
- **Tactical:** "Where Agentic AI Breaks: Edge Cases, Governance, and Human-in-the-Loop" — failure scenarios; patterns for guardrails and approvals

### Week 8
- **Flagship:** "Interview Case Ready: How I'd Evaluate an Agentic AI Product as a PM" — framework, metrics, mini case evaluation rubric; example answers
- **Tactical:** "Build vs Buy: Should Startups Roll Their Own Agent Framework?" — trade-offs; speed, control, data, talent; public case study examples

## Cluster 3: "AI for Strategy, Finance, and Ops" (Month 3, Weeks 9–12)

Positioning: Blend of accounting/finance background with PM and AI.

### Week 9
- **Flagship:** "How Finance and Strategy Teams Are Actually Using AI in 2026" — forecasting, scenario modeling, regulatory reporting, analytics copilots; ROI and limits
- **Tactical:** "AI-Driven Management Dashboards: From Raw Data to Executive Narrative" — pipeline: ingest → analyze → narrative deck; automated vs curated

### Week 10
- **Flagship:** "Using AI to Speed Up Case Prep and Consulting-Style Slides" — step-by-step from case prompt to model to storyline to slides
- **Tactical:** "Can AI Do Your Financial Modeling? A Realistic Stress Test" — build/audit a model using AI; where it helps vs fails

### Week 11
- **Flagship:** "Agentic AI for Back-Office Ops: AP, Compliance, and Reporting" — AP processing, regulatory reporting; agentic workflow with KPIs
- **Tactical:** "The Future CFO Stack: How Today's MBAs Should Think About AI Tools" — tools for analytics, planning, reporting; vision of AI-augmented finance stack

### Week 12
- **Flagship:** "What 3 Months of Publishing on AI Taught Me (Traffic, Experiments, and Pivots)" — analytics, which posts worked, how AI search/Overviews affected results; plan for next quarter
- **Tactical:** "From Blog to Product: Turning My Best-Performing Posts into a Cohort or Playbook" — identify best cluster; sketch paid cohort/mini-course/playbook; waitlist CTA
